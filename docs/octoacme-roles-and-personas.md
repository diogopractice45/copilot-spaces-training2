# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Quality Assurance / Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads define and maintain quality standards, own test strategy, and validate that deliverables meet acceptance criteria and quality expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Define quality standards and acceptance criteria frameworks
- Create and maintain test plans, test cases, and automated test suites
- Validate features against acceptance criteria before release
- Identify and prioritize defects and quality risks
- Collaborate with developers on testability and design
- Report on quality metrics and test coverage

### Goals
- Prevent quality regressions and production defects
- Reduce time to quality validation
- Build team confidence in release readiness

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and review meetings
- Quality metrics and defect reports
- Test coverage and automation strategy discussions

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Collaborate on test design, testability, and bug fixes
- **Product Managers:** Refine acceptance criteria clarity
- **Project Managers:** Report quality blockers and release readiness

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide strategic technical direction, guide architectural decisions, and ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with long-term technology strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Guide technical design and architecture decisions
- Review technical approaches for feasibility and risk
- Mentor developers on best practices and design patterns
- Identify and propose mitigation for technical debt
- Evaluate technology choices and dependencies
- Ensure solutions meet performance, scalability, and security requirements

### Goals
- Deliver technically sound, maintainable solutions
- Reduce long-term technical debt and maintenance burden
- Enable team growth through mentorship and knowledge sharing

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code review feedback and mentoring
- Technical risk identification and mitigation planning

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Provide technical guidance and code review input
- **Product Managers:** Influence feasibility and approach trade-offs
- **Project Managers:** Escalate technical risks and complexity estimates

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors represent business priorities, provide decision authority on scope and resources, and remove organizational blockers to project success.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria
- Provide budget and resource approval
- Make scope trade-off decisions
- Remove organizational and cross-team blockers
- Communicate project status to broader leadership
- Provide feedback on deliverables and business alignment

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver business value
- Maintain executive alignment and support
- Reduce organizational friction and blockers

### Typical Communication
- Monthly or quarterly status updates
- Decision gate reviews and approval meetings
- Escalation resolution and blocker removal

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Project Managers:** Provide decisions, approvals, and blocker removal
- **Product Managers:** Align on business priorities and success metrics
- **Developers:** May provide feedback on prototypes or demos

---

## DevOps / Infrastructure Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps and Infrastructure Engineers design, build, and maintain deployment infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and operational systems to enable reliable, automated release and monitoring.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipeline automation
- Manage deployment infrastructure and environments
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting
- Define deployment procedures and rollback strategies
- Manage secrets, access controls, and security configurations
- Improve deployment reliability and speed

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Reduce deployment risk and mean time to recovery
- Improve operational observability and incident response

### Typical Communication
- Release planning and deployment coordination
- Infrastructure and deployment pipeline reviews
- Incident response and post-mortems

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Support application deployment, environment setup, and troubleshooting
- **Project Managers:** Coordinate release scheduling and deployment windows
- **QA/Testing Lead:** Provide test environment infrastructure and deployment verification
